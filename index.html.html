üîçHow Do We Find Oil?

Finding oil is like being a detective, but instead of looking for clues in a room, you're looking for clues buried miles underground!

Scientists use cool technology like seismic waves - they create small earthquakes and listen to the echoes to "see" what's underground. It's like using sonar, but for rocks!

üîÑThe Extraction Process Step-by-Step

Let's follow oil's journey from deep underground all the way to your gas tank!

üé¨ Interactive Process Simulator

Click to start the step-by-step oil extraction process!

Step 1: Site Preparation

Clear land, build roads, and set up massive drilling equipment.

Step 2: Drilling

Using rotary drill bits (like giant corkscrews) to bore holes deep underground.

Step 3: Casing Installation

Installing steel pipes to prevent cave-ins and protect groundwater.

Step 4: Oil Flow

Oil flows up naturally at first, then we use pumps and injection techniques.

Step 5: Transportation

Moving oil via pipelines, trucks, trains, or ships to refineries.

Step 6: Refining

Heating crude oil and separating it into gasoline, diesel, plastics, and more!
